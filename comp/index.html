Challenges Faced

⚠Payment Delays

  • Delays in government DBT payments
  • Slow response from BOAT on escalation cases

⚠Awareness Gaps

  • Lack of awareness among supervisors about NAPS/NATS processes
  • Candidates unaware of Aadhaar seeding → Beneficiary ID delays

⚠Process Bottlenecks

  • Contract approval delays impacting payroll timelines
  • Documentation issues causing rework

⚠Coordination Issues

  • Portal errors disrupting workflow
  • Coordination gaps between teams and clients

Key Learnings

🔍

Document Verification

Complete document verification before onboarding to prevent downstream rework and delays

📅

Weekly Contract Review

Conduct weekly reviews of all pending contracts to avoid last-minute rushes and payroll impact

📈

DBT Tracker

Maintain a dedicated tracker for NAPS DBT payments to identify and escalate gaps early

Areas of Improvement

Training & Awareness

  • Train supervisors and field officers on Aadhaar seeding, eKYC, and NAPS/NATS processes
  • Regular refresher sessions for site-level teams

Data Accuracy

  • Ensure accurate entry of candidate data at source
  • Implement verification checkpoints before portal submission

SOP Creation

  • Create clear SOPs for all stages: registration → contract → stipend → termination → assessment
  • Standardize checklists for each stage

Tracking & Monitoring

  • Maintain a tracking sheet for NAPS DBT payments with status columns
  • Weekly status reports to management
